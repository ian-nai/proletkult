Welcome! Click the links on either side to view the journal content, or use the navigation above to explore the site.

This site collects the complete runs of two journals released by the Proletkult organization, Горн (Furnace) and Пролетарская Культура (Proletariat Culture), and presents them as PDFs (with OCRed and translated text) viewable online and available as downloads. The texts extracted from the PDFs (and their translations into English) can also be downloaded as .txt files on the journals' respective pages.
