This site aims to highlight the history of the Proletkult organization in the Soviet Union, making the full runs of two of its journals available as easily browseable and downloadable PDFs. The txt files from both the OCRed original texts and their translations (performed using the Google Translate API) are provided in the hope that they may be of use to anyone interested in performing textual analysis on the journals.

The site was created by Ian Goodale, European Studies and Linguistics Librarian and Digital Scholarship Specialist at the University of Texas at Austin.
